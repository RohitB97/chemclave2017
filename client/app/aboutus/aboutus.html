ChemClave, IIT Madras is one of India's leading Annual Chemical Engineering Symposium happening today. We call it our annual celebration of the Spirit of chemical Engineering rather than just some annual technical fest. With a participation from more than 250+ colleges we provide an opportunity to all the budding chemical Engineers to compete, exchange ideas, collaborate and learn. We incorporate knowledgeable and fun-filled competitions, lectures series, workshops on simulation softwares, problem presentations and much more.

Born in 2008, with the motto of promoting technology, scientific thinking and innovation in the field of Chemical Engineering, ChemClave has passed 9 golden editions and today it is successfully planning towards its 10th edition. Department Of Chemical Engineering, IIT Madras is proud to present the 10th edition of its Annual Technical Festival ChemClave in March 2017.
