Ambassador

A Chemclave Campus ambassador is the 'Face of Chemclave' in his college-exclusive handpicked students from different colleges across the country who helps students in their colleges to know about how they can live their dream careers through opportunities on chemclave.

If you think you are awesome, have great networking skills, an envious social networking, you’re just the person we’re looking for. Be a campus ambassador and join our Chemclave group

DUTIES :

  • Participate and promote – encourage people from your college to participate in our events and workshops

  • Execute on ground publicity of Chemclave's events via posters and your networking skills

  • Update the students regularly about Chemclave's activities through mails and other modes of communication

  • Share the latest activities of Chemclave through whichever social media common in your college

  • Bridge the gap between your college participants, authorities and team Chemclave for exchange of queries and information
